content editing

We examine the document for continuity errors, contextual errors, chronological confusion, contradictions, inconsistencies, advice on first lines (hooks), discrepancies in plot, theme development subplot integration into story line, argument thread, dialogue, etc.

copy editing

We will provide verification and correction of spelling, punctuation, grammar...

copy editing

We will provide verification and correction of spelling, punctuation,grammar, parts of speech, perspective, tense, pronoun matching, sentence structure, missing words, paragraph length, word choices (repetition), the general structure of the language and the language of the text. All ambiguous corrections shall be accompanied by notes, which gently explain why the correction was needed and the rule for that correction. We reference proof to standards (such as CMoS). This is the highest level of editing you can receive and you can have confidence that your writing will have the most professional appearance possible.

editorial assessment

This is usually the first step in the editing process for a manuscript.

Submissions

Blog

If you register for our blog, we will acquire your email address. We will never sell or rent your email address, and utilize it only for the purpose you provided it. You may request to unsubscribe at any time and we will remove your address from our database.

No Cookies

Unlike most web sites, TheLetterWorks.com does not use cookies to track site usage. A cookie is a tiny data file which resides on your computer. Depending upon your web-browser, you may view the information stored by Viewing Cookies, usually in a location with the options or preferences.

TheLetterWorks.com does not store sensitive data on your computer or in cookies.

Information about your computer

Due to the communications standards on the Internet, when you visit TheLetterWorks.com, we automatically receive the URL of the site from which you came and the site to which you are going when you leave TheLetterWorks.com. We also receive the Internet Protocol (IP) address of your computer (or the proxy server you use to access the World Wide Web), your computer operating system and type of web browser you are using, email patterns, as well as the name of your ISP. This information may be used to analyze overall trends to help us improve TheLetterWorks.com service. The linkage between your IP address and your personally identifiable information is never shared with third-parties without your permission except when required by law.

Third Party Privacy

TheLetterWorks.com may does not use third party plug-ins that track you. If you choose to use PayPal or Stripe for payments, these systems may track your for fraud protection. If you do not wish this tracking, please provide payment via mail.

We do not store credit card or PayPal information on our site.
